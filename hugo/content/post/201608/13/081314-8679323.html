傅園慧在外媒也火啦！外媒的翻譯是 mystic energy，primordial powers，prehistorical powers，大意是“遠古力量 / 神祕的精神力量”。

我做爲本家，也作爲一個寫了好多“怎麼學英語”答案的人，怎麼着也得趁這個機會透過傅園慧的外媒報道普及幾個能現學現用的英文表達。

Overnight social media star/overnight sensation：大火的明星；go viral 火了（動詞）

The enthusiasm of one Chinese swimmer after winning a bronze has made her an overnight social media star. 一箇中國游泳運動員贏得銅牌後的熱烈表現讓她成爲中國社交媒體紅人。- BBC

Online meme 表情包；fete 追捧；spoof 搞笑模仿（動詞）

Fans feted her as “Primordial Girl” in online memes and viral videos spoofing her exuberant expressions. 粉絲追捧她爲“洪荒女孩”，模仿她熱烈的表情做了一系列表情包和紅遍網絡的視頻。- WSJ

Flooded with：被刷屏

Weibo was flooded with emoticons made of Fu. 微博被傅園慧的表情刷屏。- SCMP

Heartfelt 真誠的（同 authentic，candid）；also-ran 落敗的（例句中引申改爲 also-swam）

Two heartfelt interviews was all it took to transform 20-year-old Chinese swimmer Fu Yuanhui from also-swam to social media star. 兩個真誠的採訪讓 20 歲的中國游泳運動員傅園慧從落敗者一下變成了社交媒體紅人。- Variety

Expressive：表情豐富的；barometer for：...的體現

Her expressive face is the perfect barometer for how we feel watching the Olympic Games. 她豐富的的表情完美體現了我們觀看奧運會感受。- NYMag

Endear 吸引（粉絲）；make animated faces 做表情；exuberance 充滿能量、激情（名詞）；

It is her exuberance out of the water that has endeared her to fans. Rather than mere happiness at her performances, Ms. Fu, 20, exhibits something closer to pure joy, bouncing around and making animated faces. 她出水後的熱情吸引了衆多粉絲。20 歲的傅園慧展現的不僅是她對自己發揮的滿意，還有一種接近純粹快樂的東西。她跳來跳去，做各種表情。- NYT

Gasp：倒吸一口氣

Fu, who had thought that she had failed to win a medal, gasped joyfully when told by a television reporter that she had actually won bronze. 以爲自己沒得獎牌的傅園慧在聽記者說自己其實得了銅牌後，開心得倒吸了一口氣。- Reuters

Deadpan：假裝嚴肅地說笑話；Ready：詼諧的

She deadpanned that her “arms are too short” after learning that she had finished just one-hundredth of a second off the silver-medal winning time. 在聽說自己以 0.01 秒的劣勢輸給了銀牌得主時，傅園慧嚴肅地說，我的手臂太短了。- NYT
Fu had a ready explanation about why she couldn’t top third place on Tuesday. Her arms, she said, were “too short.” 對於自己週二的比賽爲什麼沒能比第三名更進一步，傅園慧有個詼諧的解釋。我的手臂太短了，她說。- The Guardian

寫下面這段的記者應該在發文章的時候笑了好久吧...

Return to the spotlight 重新回到人們關注中心；bop 激動地跳來跳去

Ms. Fu will return to the Olympic spotlight on Saturday when she swims in the 4x100-meter medley relay. She was part of the Chinese team that won the event last year at the World Championships. Afterward she stood holding her medal with her teammates, and as they smiled determinedly, she bopped around like a human exclamation point. 傅園慧週六將參加 4x100 米接力的比賽，那時她將重新回到人們關注中心。她是去年世錦賽這個項目冠軍隊的成員。她當時和隊友一起拿着獎牌站上領獎臺，而當隊友微笑的時候，她像感嘆號一樣激動地跳來跳去。- NYT

最後這句不需要解釋：

This is Fu Yuanhui’s Olympics. We’re just watching it. 這是傅園慧的奧運會。我們都是觀衆。 - Huffington Post

如果你只能記住一個詞，請一定要學會 authentic，表示“真性情”，true to one's own personality, spirit, or character。
